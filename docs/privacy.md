# Bao mat va rieng tu

Cong cu nay chi doc thong tin de lap bao cao. Cong cu khong kich hoat, khong
go key, khong sua registry, khong xoa file va khong thay doi cau hinh may.

## Du lieu duoc thu thap

- Thoi diem trich xuat, ten may, tai khoan dang dang nhap, domain/workgroup.
- Thong tin Windows: ten he dieu hanh, phien ban, build, kien truc, trang thai
  ban quyen, partial product key.
- Thong tin Office neu co `OSPP.VBS`: ten san pham, trang thai license, 5 ky tu
  cuoi cua key Office.
- Thong tin phan cung: hang san xuat, model, CPU, RAM, mainboard, BIOS, o dia,
  dia chi MAC dang hoat dong.
- Chi bao nghi van tu ten ung dung, tien trinh, service, scheduled task, startup
  entry va ten file trong vung quet gioi han.

## Du lieu duoc che

- Username duoc thay bang `<REDACTED_USER>`.
- Dia chi MAC duoc che cac byte o giua.
- Serial number chi hien 3 ky tu dau va 4 ky tu cuoi.
- Duong dan co username se duoc che username.
- Cac gia tri giong full product key se bi che.

## Du lieu khong bao gio thu thap

- Full Windows product key.
- Full Office product key.
- Full OEM OA3 key.
- Noi dung file.
- Mat khau, token, cookie, noi dung email, noi dung tai lieu ca nhan.
- Du lieu tu toan bo o `C:\`; file scan chi quet vung gioi han va chi xem ten
  file/duong dan.

## Cam ket read-only

Cong cu chi thuc hien truy van doc va lenh chan doan read-only. Cong cu khong
xoa, cach ly, sua, kich hoat, huy kich hoat, cai key, go key, dung service,
sua scheduled task hay thay doi trang thai license.
