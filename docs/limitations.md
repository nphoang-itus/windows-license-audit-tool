# Gioi han cua cong cu

## Trang thai kich hoat khong phai bang chung phap ly tuyet doi

Windows hoac Office bao `Licensed` chi cho biet trang thai kich hoat ma he
thong dang ghi nhan. Ket qua nay khong phai bang chung tuyet doi ve quyen so
huu license hop le, hoa don mua hang, hop dong volume license hay dieu kien su
dung.

## KMS can ngu canh to chuc

KMS la co che kich hoat phu hop trong nhieu moi truong doanh nghiep. Mot may
dung KMS can duoc doi chieu voi ngu canh to chuc: ten KMS host, domain, chinh
sach CNTT va hop dong license. KMS host khong nam trong danh sach tin cay chi
la dau hieu can xem xet, khong phai ket luan tuyet doi.

## Chi bao nghi van co the sai

Tu khoa nghi van trong ten app, process, service, task hoac file co the tao
false positive. Vi du: file ghi chu, bo cai cu, cong cu quan tri noi bo, hoac
ten trung voi keyword. Moi chi bao nghi van can duoc kiem tra thu cong truoc
khi dua ra ket luan.

## Gioi han ky thuat

- Neu WMI/CIM, registry, scheduled task hoac thu muc bi chan quyen truy cap,
  cong cu se ghi warning va tiep tuc.
- Office chi duoc doc qua cac vi tri `OSPP.VBS` pho bien. Ban Office khac co
  the khong duoc phat hien.
- File scan mac dinh bi gioi han do sau va vi tri de tranh quet toan bo may.
